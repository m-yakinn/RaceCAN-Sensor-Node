# Parts List Draft

## Required

1. Two microcontroller boards
2. Two MCP2515 CAN modules
3. Two 120 ohm resistors for CAN termination
4. Breadboard
5. Jumper wires
6. 10k thermistor
7. 10k potentiometer
8. Assorted resistors
9. LEDs
10. Push buttons
11. 12 V power source
12. Buck converter module
13. Multimeter

## Optional

1. Logic analyzer
2. Oscilloscope access
3. USB CAN adapter
4. JST connectors
5. Screw terminals
6. KiCad PCB order from JLCPCB or PCBWay
